Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice

This is a rice dish prepared with a number of ingredients. Many countries in the world serve this delicacy.

It can be taken with many beverages also. Try it now!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Add some amount of rice to a considerate amount of water and cook for sometime
  2. Semi-fry some vegetables of choice in a cooking pan
  3. Add some eggs, and then transfer the rice into the pan and mix well
  4. Add some soy sauce
  5. Mix for a while for a few minutes
  6. Your recipe is ready
