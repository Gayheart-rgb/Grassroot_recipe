Pancake Recipe

Pancakes

This is sweet-flavored recipe made from a number of ingredients. Many countries in the world serve this delicacy.

It can be taken with many beverages. Try it now!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Pineapple flavor essence
  • Nutmeg
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add some amount of milk to a considerate amount of water
  2. Add some flour and mix
  3. Add some eggs, and shred of nutmegs and mix
  4. Add some sugar, essence and mix well for a fine texture
  5. Set fire and place a pan on it and add some oil
  6. Pour some of the prepared mixture and fry for a few minutes. then your sweet delicious pancake is ready for consumption
