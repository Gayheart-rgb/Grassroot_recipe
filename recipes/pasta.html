Pasta Recipe

Pasta

Who does not like noodles? Easy to prepare, and easy to eat. Made from spaghetti. Many countries in the world serve this delicacy.

It can be taken with many beverages. Try it now!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Add some amount of spaghetti to a bowl of water and cook for a few minutes
  2. Drain water off
  3. Add some vegetables and salt, and mix
  4. Mix for a few minutes. then your sweet delicious pasta is ready for consumption
